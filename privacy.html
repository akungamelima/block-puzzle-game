🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

📅 Effective date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BlockPuzzle.fun. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

By accessing and using BlockPuzzle.fun, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect very minimal information to provide the best gaming experience:

  • Local Data (localStorage): Your high score is stored locally on your device using the Web Storage API. This data is never sent to our servers.
  • Anonymous Analytics: If we use third-party analytics services in the future, only anonymous data such as visitor counts, pages visited, and session duration will be collected.
  • Server Logs: Our servers automatically record IP addresses, browser types, and pages visited for security and performance purposes. This data is temporary and not personally identifiable.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Save your game progress and high score locally on your device
  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our service
  • Analyze usage patterns anonymously to improve the game

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

BlockPuzzle.fun uses localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store your game score on your local device. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

You can delete this local data at any time through your browser settings (Settings → Privacy → Clear Site Data).

5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of BlockPuzzle.fun or other users

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Your game score data is stored only on your own device.

7. Children's Privacy

BlockPuzzle.fun is a safe game for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game requires no account registration or personal data submission of any kind.

8. Links to External Sites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Know what data is stored about you (in this case, only your local score on your device)
  • Delete: Remove your local score at any time through your browser settings
  • Opt Out: Disable localStorage through your browser settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • 📧 Email: privacy@blockpuzzlegame.fun
  • 🌐 Website: https://blockpuzzlegame.fun
