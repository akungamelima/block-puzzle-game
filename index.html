How to Play

Learn to play in 4 easy steps

🎯
1

Select a Block

Click or tap one of the 3 available blocks shown at the bottom of the board.

📍
2

Place the Block

Click an empty area on the 10×10 board to place the block at your desired position.

💥
3

Clear Rows & Columns

Fill an entire horizontal row or vertical column to clear it and earn points!

🏆
4

Beat Your Score

Clear multiple lines at once for a combo bonus! Game over when no block can fit on the board.

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • 🔥 Clear multiple lines at once for a combo bonus
  • 📊 Always think a few moves ahead before placing a block
  • 🎯 Keep the board from getting too full — leave room for big pieces
  • ⚡ Blocks cannot be rotated, so choose your position carefully
  • 🌟 Your high score is saved automatically on your device
